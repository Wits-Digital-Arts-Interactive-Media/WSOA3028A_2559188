WEEK 4

Goal Alignment:

My Goal: To make a website that is user friendly, whose interface incites interest amongst it's users. I want my website to showcase my portfolio from throughout the semester while also giving them insight into who I am as a creative.
The User: The user should be able to find their way seamlessly through my website, whether they are taking a look at my portfolio work or just browsing.
My Content: My animation assignments done in Blender, my website code and/or planning and essays I have written throughout the semester so far.

Input Controls: Buttons with a short description, leading to further information like a blog post for a specific week.
Navigation Components: A navigation bar will be present on each page, allowing users easy access through my website.
Information Components: I will possibly use icons as well as tooltips.

Reflection on my Website:

The making of this website was not as daunting as I thought it would be. It was my first time learning HTML, but I found it a lot easier to grasp compared to when I learned C# last year. The MDN developer page really helped in grasping the theory and using it in a practical way. Semantics are one thing I still need to make a habit of using within my website.

I am proud that I slowly managed to bring most of the ideas for my website to life. I am proud that I have memorized and practiced most of the essential elements and semantics of the HTML language. The basics are slowly becoming second nature to me and I am glad that I have put in the effort.

Going forward, I would like to change my process when designing my website. Though I have made some progress, I believe there is still more I need to learn in terms of the overall look and feel of my website. All of this will come with more learning.
